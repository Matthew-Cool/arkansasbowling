The University of Arkansas

Why Choose the University of Arkansas?

The University of Arkansas is the flagship university of the state and one of the largest schools in the region. Located in Fayetteville, it enrolls more than 34,000 students and offers a vibrant SEC campus atmosphere known for its traditions and school spirit.

Senior Walk Tradition

One of the most unique traditions at the University of Arkansas is the Senior Walk. Since 1876, the names of every graduating student have been etched into the sidewalks across campus, creating a lasting legacy for Razorbacks who graduate from the university.

Learn About Senior Walk

Campus Life in Fayetteville

Life at the University of Arkansas is built around strong traditions, school spirit, and the excitement of SEC athletics. Razorback game days bring the campus and community together, creating an atmosphere that is unique to large universities in the Southeastern Conference.
