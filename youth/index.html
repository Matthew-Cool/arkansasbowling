Youth Bowling Development

The Arkansas Bowling Foundation is committed to growing the sport of bowling across Arkansas by supporting youth athletes and programs.

We provide opportunities for young bowlers to develop their skills, compete at higher levels, and pursue their academic and athletic goals through scholarships, grants, and community support.

Scholarships

The Arkansas Bowling Foundation plans to offer scholarship opportunities to youth bowlers who demonstrate dedication both on the lanes and in the classroom.

These scholarships will help athletes continue their education while pursuing collegiate bowling opportunities and other future goals.

High School Team Grants

We aim to support Arkansas high school bowling programs through grants that help cover equipment costs, tournament fees, uniforms, and other program expenses.

By investing in local teams, we hope to strengthen the future of competitive bowling throughout the state.
